Custom Photo Stainless Steel Urns for Ashes

These custom photo stainless steel urns are more than just a container for ashes—they are a heartfelt tribute to your loved one. With a personalized photo and engraving, each urn is uniquely designed to reflect the memories you hold dear. Crafted from high-quality stainless steel, they are long-lasting, water-resistant, and built to preserve your loved one’s remains with care. The sleek, modern look of these urns makes them a beautiful addition to your home or a meaningful keepsake for remembrance.

Why Choose These Urns?

  • Customizable with cherished photos and meaningful text to create a lasting personal memory.
  • Premium stainless steel for durability and rust resistance, ensuring your urn stands the test of time.
  • Threaded screw-lid with rubber gasket for secure closure, keeping ashes safe and protected from the elements.
  • Compact size ideal for sharing ashes among family members, making it easier to spread the remembrance.
  • Includes a velvet pouch for elegant memorial display, adding a touch of class and respect to your keepsake.

Whether for a cremation, funeral, or simply as a memorial, these mini urns offer a thoughtful way to honor your loved one’s legacy. They are versatile and suitable for holding ashes, dried flowers, soil from a grave, or a lock of hair, making them a meaningful symbol for any occasion. Their elegant design and secure construction make them the perfect choice for both home display and lasting tribute.
