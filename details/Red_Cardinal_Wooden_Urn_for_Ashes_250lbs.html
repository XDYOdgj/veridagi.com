Red Cardinal Wooden Urn

A beautifully crafted wooden urn featuring a striking red cardinal design, perfect for honoring the memory of a loved one with a lasting tribute.

Elegant and Meaningful

This urn provides a dignified and meaningful way to remember a cherished loved one. The intricate red cardinal design symbolizes love, devotion, and remembrance, making it a beautiful addition to any memorial setting.

Built to Last

Handcrafted from durable composite wood, this urn is not only long-lasting but also eco-friendly. The UV printing ensures that the design remains vivid and intact, giving it a timeless appeal that endures for years.

Designed for Comfort

Its sophisticated finish and expert craftsmanship reflect a deep respect for the departed. Whether placed in a home, garden, or as part of a funeral arrangement, this urn offers a serene and meaningful tribute.
