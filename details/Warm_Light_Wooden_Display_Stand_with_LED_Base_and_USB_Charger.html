Elegant Wooden Display Stand with LED Light Base

This beautifully crafted wooden display stand brings a touch of natural elegance to your space. Featuring a soft, warm LED crystal light base, it enhances the visual appeal of any transparent decor pieces, such as glass sculptures, crystal vases, or resin art. The built-in USB wall charger ensures convenient and energy-efficient power, with a 5V1A output and CCC certification for reliable performance and safety.

Key Features

  • LED crystal light base for elegant illumination
  • Adjustable stand for stable placement of spherical or rectangular items
  • Warm light tone enhances the look of glass, resin, or crystal decor
  • Compact USB wall charger with 5V1A output and CCC certification
  • Eco-friendly materials ensure durability and safe use

Whether you're looking to elevate your home decor with a refined accent or showcase your favorite art pieces, this stand offers a perfect blend of style and practicality. Its warm light tone creates a welcoming glow, ideal for use in living rooms, bedrooms, or entryways. The wooden construction adds a timeless, organic feel, while the adjustable stand ensures your items are displayed securely and beautifully.
