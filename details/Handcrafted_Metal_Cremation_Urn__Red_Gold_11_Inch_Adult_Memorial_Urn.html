Honoring Your Loved One with Elegance and Meaning

This handcrafted metal cremation urn is a beautiful and meaningful way to honor your loved one. Its timeless design combines grace and strength, making it a perfect addition to any home or memorial space.

Thoughtful Design for Lasting Memories

With an elegant red and gold color scheme, this urn adds a sophisticated touch to any setting. It's meticulously crafted with unique variations, ensuring each piece is a one-of-a-kind tribute to your loved one's life.

Built to Last and Carefully Designed

The durable lightweight metal construction ensures long-lasting use, making it a cherished keepsake that can be passed down through generations. Its secure screw-top lid offers safe and sealed storage, protecting the ashes from moisture and damage.

Versatile Display and Practical Functionality

This urn is designed to hold up to 220 lbs of ashes, making it ideal for adult remains.
